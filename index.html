Core Features

⚡

Idle Qi Collection

Your spirit beasts gather Qi automatically, even offline. Progress never stops — claim your rewards anytime.

🥚

Hatch & Discover

Incubate eggs across six rarity tiers. Each hatch is a surprise — from gentle Common beasts to legendary Mythic creatures.

🔄

Merge to Evolve

Combine two beasts of the same tier to create a higher-tier creature. Strategize your merges for maximum power.

🏔️

Explore Five Realms

Journey from the Mortal Realm to the Great Wild. Each realm unlocks new beasts, bonuses, and exploration rewards.

🍖

Feed & Nurture

Keep your beasts well-fed for peak Qi production. Hungry beasts work slower — visit regularly to care for your collection.

📖

Complete Your Bestiary

Track every beast you've discovered. The ultimate goal: collect all 55 entries and become a true Beast Master.
