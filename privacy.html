Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 26, 2026

Shanhai: Spirit Era is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information. This policy explains what limited data may be processed when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personally identifiable information. Specifically:

  • No name, email address, or phone number
  • No account or login credentials
  • No precise location data
  • No device identifiers beyond what is required for core functionality
  • No browsing history or usage analytics

All game progress, beast collections, and settings are stored locally on your device using Apple's standard storage APIs (UserDefaults). This data never leaves your device and is deleted when you uninstall the app.

2. Third-Party Services

Our app integrates the following third-party services:

Google AdMob

We display advertisements through Google AdMob to keep the game free to play. Google AdMob may collect certain device information for ad personalization and measurement. The data collected by Google is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

Google User Messaging Platform (UMP)

We use Google's User Messaging Platform to present consent dialogs to users in regions governed by GDPR and similar privacy regulations. This allows you to control how your data is used for advertising purposes.

Apple StoreKit

In-app purchases are processed by Apple through StoreKit. We do not have access to your payment information. All transactions are handled securely by Apple and are subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.

3. Advertising & Tracking

We do not track users across apps and websites owned by other companies. Our app does not use any custom analytics, tracking pixels, or fingerprinting technologies.

Google AdMob may use the device's advertising identifier (IDFA) if you have provided consent through Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework. You can manage or revoke this permission at any time in your device's Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking.

You may also purchase the Remove Ads in-app option to eliminate all advertising and associated data processing entirely.

4. Children's Privacy

Our app is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal data through our app, please contact us immediately and we will take steps to remove such information.

5. Data Security

Since all game data is stored locally on your device, no external servers or databases are involved. We recommend keeping your device's operating system up to date for the latest security protections.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal data
  • Restrict or object to data processing
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority

Because we do not collect or store personal data, these rights are inherently satisfied by design. For data collected by Google AdMob, please refer to Google's privacy tools and your device's ad privacy settings.

7. International Users

This app is distributed globally. If you access the app from outside your country of residence, please be aware that limited data processing by third-party services (such as Google AdMob) may occur in various jurisdictions. These services maintain appropriate safeguards for international data transfers.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
